# Survey Calculator Suite - Quick Analysis Summary

## Current Application Features

### 📍 Coordinate Converter App
**Capabilities:**
- ✅ 9,000+ EPSG coordinate reference systems
- ✅ Smart CRS auto-detection from coordinate values
- ✅ Single & bulk point conversion (9000+ at once)
- ✅ Geoid height conversion (ellipsoidal ↔ orthometric)
- ✅ Multiple input formats: CSV, TXT, WKT, UTM, GeoJSON, GPX, KML, XLSX, Shapefile ZIP
- ✅ Multiple export formats: CSV, GeoJSON, KML, GPX, XLSX, WKT, DXF, Shapefile
- ✅ 2D map visualization (Leaflet)
- ✅ 3D Earth visualization (THREE.js)
- ✅ DMS (Degrees/Minutes/Seconds) input/output
- ✅ Zone warnings for regional systems (UTM, CC, GK, etc.)
- ✅ Automatic geoid grid selection by location
- ✅ Format detection & conversion
- ✅ Language-agnostic calculations

### 📏 Distance & Bearing Tool  
**Capabilities:**
- ✅ 5 distance calculations: Slope, Horizontal, Grid, Ground, Geodesic
- ✅ Scale factors & convergence angles
- ✅ Forward/Reverse azimuths
- ✅ Vincenty's formulas for accuracy
- ✅ Elevation/Height support
- ✅ UTM projection integration
- ✅ Map-based interactive point picking
- ✅ Angle units: Degrees & Gradians
- ✅ Real-time calculations
- ✅ Zone auto-detection
